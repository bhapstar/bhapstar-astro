FAQ

What print sizes are available?

Prints are available in A3, A4 or A5 sizes.
A3 will be: 29.7 × 42.0 cm / 11.7 × 16.5 in
A4 will be: 21.0 × 29.7 cm / 8.3 × 11.7 in
A5 will be: 14.8 × 21.0 cm / 5.8 × 8.3 in

What paper is used for printing?

Printing will be on premium glossy 200gsm photo-quality paper with an advanced waterproof coating - keeping prints clean, sharp and protected from moisture and fingerprints over time.

Can I have my print framed?

Yes, a high quality black frame can be supplied. Other frame colours are available by request.

How will the prints be shipped?

Unframed prints will be sent in a secure rolled mailing tube. Framed prints will be securely packed with corner protectors.

How does the digital download work?

The high-resolution digital file will be emailed to you directly.

Do the prints come with the description of what they are?

Yes, with each print you will also receive a small printout on high quality card of its title and description, as available on the Gallery page .

Do you ship internationally?

It depends on destination and courier options. Tell me your location in the enquiry and I'll confirm what's possible.

Can I request a specific crop or aspect ratio?

Yes, in many cases I can prepare a crop that suits your chosen size. Mention it in the notes.

What if you re-image or reprocess something I've acquired?

If I release a significantly updated version of that image, I'll contact you with the option to acquire the updated version.

Do the prints or digital file come with the watermark?

No, the image you receive (whether by print or digital file) will be without the watermark. The watermark is placed only on the images you see on the website to protect them from unauthorised download.
